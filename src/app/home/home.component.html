What?
DMO it's an analytical structured report which describes the movie screenplay in the most possible detailed way.
DMO editor is designed specifically for analysing movie screenplay. It helps you to understand the structure of the story.
Once you’re read or create DMO you know everything about movie. It’s not about critic evaluation it’s about screenwriter craft.
Everything in a movie tell a story. And there’s just no single comprehensive way which could completely describe movie in all its endless details.
DMO is meant to change it.

Why?
Screenplay it’s not just some text which was laid into the directors’ hands. It’s numerous variations of decisions which resulted into compelling story. Isn't it interesting?
Usually first version of screenplay and final movie are two different planets. DMO lets you to learn from it.
It’s realy hard to understand the rules of screenplay even for proffesionals. Dmo is new method of understanding the Principles of Screenwriting.
DMO might explain you why some movies stay in the cinema history and others are forgotten next day after release.
