DMO stands for Decomposed Movie Object. It’s an analytical structured report which describes the story in the most possible detailed way.
New method of understanding
the Principles of Screenwriting.
DMO editor is designed specifically for analysing movie screenplay. It helps you to understand the structure of the story.
What?
Everything in a movie tell a story. And there’s just no single comprehensive way which could completely describe the movie in all it’s endless details DMO is meant to fix it.
It’s not about critic evaluation it’s about screenwriter professionalism. Once you’re read or create a DMO you know
everything about the movie.
